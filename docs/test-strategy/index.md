# Test Strategy

Decisions about what to test, how much to test, and when to stop.

Test strategy is the part of QA work that is hardest to make visible and most expensive to get wrong. A good strategy focuses engineering effort where failure would matter most. A bad strategy produces either false confidence from high coverage numbers, or uncovered risk from scattered effort.
