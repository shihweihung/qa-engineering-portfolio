# QA Strategy

Notes on quality engineering fundamentals, test strategy, and how to build effective QA processes.

## Core topics

- **Test strategy** — how to scope what to test, what to skip, and how to communicate trade-offs
- **Automation design** — when to automate, how to structure test suites, and how to keep them maintainable
- **Risk-based testing** — prioritizing coverage where failure impact is highest
- **Quality metrics** — what to measure, what not to measure, and how to avoid vanity metrics
- **Tooling decisions** — selecting and retiring tools based on team needs, not hype
