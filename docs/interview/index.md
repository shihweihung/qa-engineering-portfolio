# Interview Preparation

Notes and frameworks for QA Lead interviews.

## What to expect in a QA Lead interview

QA Lead interviews typically cover four areas:

- **Technical depth** — test strategy, automation design, tool selection, and debugging approach
- **Leadership and influence** — how you build quality culture, mentor engineers, and push back on bad process
- **Cross-functional communication** — working with PMs, developers, and stakeholders under pressure
- **Judgment under ambiguity** — scoping decisions, risk trade-offs, and prioritization when time is short
